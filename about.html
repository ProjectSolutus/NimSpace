About NimSpace

NimSpace is an innovative, open-source web application designed for seamless, direct communication and sharing right in your browser. Connect with friends, family, or colleagues without relying on central servers.

Built with modern web technologies, NimSpace empowers you to interact directly, enhancing privacy and offering a more efficient way to share and connect online.

Key Features:

  • Direct Chat: Enjoy real-time conversations with connected peers.
  • Secure File Sharing: Easily share files of any size directly with others in your session.
  • Synchronized Media Playback: Watch videos or listen to music together in perfect sync, whether from a URL, a local file, or even a torrent magnet link.
  • Privacy-First Design: All data transfers happen directly between users, ensuring your information remains private and secure.

Our Mission:

At NimSpace, our mission is to provide a robust, private, and user-friendly platform for direct online interaction. We believe in empowering you with full control over your data and communication, fostering a more connected and secure digital environment.

Open Source & Community Driven:

NimSpace is proudly an open-source project, licensed under the MIT License. This means our codebase is transparent, auditable, and open for contributions from developers worldwide. We encourage community involvement to help us grow, improve, and innovate.

Your feedback, bug reports, and contributions are highly valued! Join us in shaping the future of direct online interaction.
