About NimSpace

NimSpace is an innovative, open-source web application designed to facilitate seamless peer-to-peer communication, file sharing, and shared media experiences directly in your browser.

Built with modern web technologies, NimSpace empowers users to connect directly with friends, family, or colleagues without relying on central servers for data transfer. This enhances privacy and offers a more direct, efficient way to interact online.

Key Features:

  • Peer-to-Peer Chat: Engage in real-time conversations with connected peers.
  • Secure File Transfer: Share files of any size directly with other users in your session.
  • "Binge" Shared Media: Watch videos or listen to music together in synchronized sessions, whether from a URL, a local file, or even a torrent magnet link.
  • No Central Servers: All peer-to-peer data transfer happens directly between users, ensuring your data remains private.

Our Mission:

At NimSpace, our mission is to provide a robust, private, and user-friendly platform for direct online interaction. We believe in empowering users with control over their data and communication, fostering a more connected and secure digital environment.

Open Source & Community Driven:

NimSpace is proudly an open-source project, licensed under the MIT License. This means our codebase is transparent, auditable, and open for contributions from developers worldwide. We encourage community involvement to help us grow, improve, and innovate.

Your feedback, bug reports, and contributions are highly valued!
